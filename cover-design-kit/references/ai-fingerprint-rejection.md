# AI-Fingerprint Rejection Checklist

Inspect every illustrator deliverable. If ANY check fails, reject the delivery and specify the failing region for hand-redraw.

## Hands and Fingers
- [ ] Correct finger count
- [ ] Correct knuckle count per finger
- [ ] Thumb anatomy correct (opposable, not fused to palm)
- [ ] Hands in pose hold objects correctly (cup, book, sword handle, etc.)
- [ ] No fused or melted fingers

## Faces
- [ ] Eyes symmetric and matched
- [ ] Pupils centered and non-distorted
- [ ] Ears symmetric (both visible or both absent)
- [ ] No asymmetric features (different eye sizes, different eyebrow arcs)
- [ ] Skin does not have AI-aesthetic plastic sheen
- [ ] Expression reads intentionally — not uncanny-valley blank

## Background
- [ ] Architecture: lines meet at coherent angles; doorframes and windows are straight or intentionally curved
- [ ] Objects: teacups, bottles, candles have correct proportions and physics (no melted handles, impossible handles)
- [ ] Text within illustration (signage, book spines): readable, correctly spelled, not garbled
- [ ] Perspective consistent across the scene
- [ ] Shadow direction consistent with light source

## Overall
- [ ] No AI "shimmer" — overexposed highlights in places that don't warrant them
- [ ] No impossible textures (fabric that behaves like liquid, stone that looks foamy)
- [ ] No uncanny blends where foreground and background pixels lose distinct edges
- [ ] Image reads as hand-drawn — brush or pencil texture visible where appropriate

## If any check fails

- Reject the delivery (don't accept and "fix in Photoshop" — start at the source).
- Specify the failing region and the problem in writing.
- Ask for a hand-redrawn version of that region only, keeping the rest of the composition intact.
- Do not accept Round 3 with known AI fingerprints. That's a terminating offense at ship.

## If you ship with an AI fingerprint

- Reader communities (BookTok, r/Fantasy, r/PubTips) will flag it.
- Genre reviewers will cite it.
- Conversion will drop measurably.
- The series' brand absorbs the cost forever — you cannot recover a cover-shaming reputation in Heartland Fantasy.
