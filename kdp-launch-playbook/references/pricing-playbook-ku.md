# Heartland Fantasy KDP Select Pricing Playbook

Board default. CEO Jason approves adjustments per book.

## Book 1

- **Launch week (T-0 to T+7):** $0.99 Kindle Countdown Deal
- **Post-launch:** settle at $4.99

Rationale: $0.99 maximizes rank spike + sample downloads on Day 1. $4.99 long-term is the KU sweet spot — high enough to signal quality, under the $9.99 70%-royalty ceiling, pairs well with KENP page-reads (~$0.0045/page ≈ $1.80 for a 400-page cozy read-through, roughly matching 70% royalty on a $2.99 sale).

## Books 2–6

- Launch directly at **$4.99**. Don't train readers to wait for $0.99.

## Countdown Deals

- 7 days max per deal
- 30-day cooldown after each deal
- 30-day cooldown before next enrollment renewal
- Use for Book 1 perma-discounts during series launches (e.g., when Book 3 drops, run Book 1 Countdown at $0.99 for a week to funnel new readers into the series)

## Free Book Promotion

- 5 days per 90-day KDP Select enrollment period
- Non-rollover
- Use on Book 1 once Books 1–3 are live — free Book 1 funnels paid reads into Books 2–3

## Big Bang Strategy (Book 6 launch)

- Drop Book 1 to $0.99 for launch week
- Stack BookBub, Freebooksy, or equivalent promo
- Full-funnel sell-through Book 1 → Book 6

## Regional Overrides

Default auto-convert overprices GBP/EUR. Manually set:

| Region | USD Equivalent Price |
|---|---|
| UK (£) | £3.99 |
| EU (€) | €4.99 |
| Canada (CA$) | CA$6.49 |
| Australia (AU$) | AU$6.99 |
| India (₹) | ₹99 (aggressive — check local norms) |

**Quarterly review** — currency fluctuations and regional competitive pricing drift.

## Royalty Economics

- **70% royalty** on $2.99–$9.99 ebook prices (KDP Select)
- 35% royalty outside that range
- **KENP:** ~$0.0045–$0.0050 per page read (fluctuates monthly with Global Fund payout)
- **Break-even on KU vs. retail:** a full read-through of a 400-page cozy book in KU earns ~$1.80. A $4.99 retail sale at 70% earns ~$3.49. KU reads must exceed ~50–55% completion to match retail — Heartland Fantasy typically hits 80–95% read-through, so KU is net-positive.

## Paperback Pricing

- KDP enforces minimum based on trim + page count + print cost
- For ~90k Heartland Fantasy (~320pp, 5.25×8, cream, B&W): minimum typically ~$6.50
- **List at $14.99** — standard indie Heartland Fantasy paperback price
- Royalty: list price − print cost − 40% Amazon cut
- Paperback royalty is dessert, not the meal — the ebook + KU is the primary revenue

## Hardcover (when applicable)

- Not Phase 1
- Consider for Book 6 / box set / reader-favorite tier post Book 3 sales signal
- List $24.99–$29.99 standard indie hardcover range

## Hard Rules

- Never raise price mid-Countdown-Deal (Amazon blocks)
- Never drop price without Countdown Deal (Amazon's "Price Match" may kick in and complicate royalty calc)
- Regional pricing manual override — Never trust auto-convert for long
- Book 1 $0.99 is launch-week only; do not perma-$0.99 or it becomes a stigma price
