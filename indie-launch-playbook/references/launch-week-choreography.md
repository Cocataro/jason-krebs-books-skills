# Launch-week choreography (7 days)

Amazon's first-week algorithm gives a 7-day attention boost based on velocity. Your job is to concentrate the audience's buying action into this window.

## T-3 (Wednesday, 3 days before launch)

### Morning
- Send pre-order nudge email to the engaged segment:
  - Subject: "3 days out"
  - Body: Short, Jason-voice, warm. Pre-order link + "the fire's getting lit" tone
  - Send time: 10am user-local (MailerLite send-time-optimization if available)
- Post BookTok: "3 days" countdown video (atmospheric, no hard sell)
- Pin the /the-hearth signup link in any active community conversations

### Evening
- Check pre-order count (from KDP dashboard)
- Verify all launch-day automations in MailerLite are queued (do a dry-run)
- Verify Ben has final Amazon listing URL ready

## T-1 (Monday, 1 day before launch)

- Send "tomorrow" email to full list (not just engaged segment)
  - Subject: "Tomorrow"
  - Body: Pre-order link, one-line hook, warm sign-off
- BookTok: "tomorrow" post — 15-30 sec atmospheric
- Reddit: if genuine engagement exists in r/CozyFantasy, a low-key "my debut drops tomorrow" post from the pen persona account is acceptable (per community norms; lurk first)

## T-0 (Tuesday, launch day) — THE EVENT

**CRITICAL: concentrate the buying action.**

### 6am local
- Verify Book 1 is live on Amazon (Ben confirms)
- Verify price is $0.99 Countdown Deal (launch-week price per Board strategy)
- Verify ASIN is correctly populated everywhere the site references it
- Activate Amazon Ads campaign (if Board approved)

### 8am local
- Send launch-day email (full list)
  - Subject: "It's open"
  - Body: very short. "The door is open. / [buy link] / — Jason"
  - No detail needed; engaged list knows what this is

### 10am local
- BookTok launch post (atmospheric cover reveal with link)
- Instagram/Twitter launch post
- Reddit community share (where genuine engagement exists; NOT cross-posting blind)

### Afternoon
- Monitor initial sales / KENP
- Respond to community replies, DMs, comments personally (Jason voice)
- Nudge ARC readers who haven't posted yet: one personal message each

### Evening
- Send one more email to non-openers of the morning: resend with different subject line ("Today's the day the inn opens its doors")
- Review rank movement + download count
- Flag to Board any platform anomaly

## T+1 (Wednesday)

- Morning: quick check-in email to engaged list with any unexpected milestone ("500 downloads in 24 hours — thank you")
- BookTok: "launch day was wild" behind-the-scenes content
- Monitor Amazon Ads performance — if CPA already >$4, pause + investigate; if <$2, consider bid increase
- Aggregate first reviews as they post; thank reviewers personally

## T+3 (Friday)

- Review-request email to ARC team:
  - Subject: "If you had a moment"
  - Body: Soft ask — "If you've finished and have 60 seconds, a review would mean the world. Link below. No pressure if you're not there yet."
- Social push: share first reviews as story content (with permission) and pin to profile
- Begin first post-launch Ledger post (momentum content)

## T+5 to T+7 (Sunday-Tuesday)

- Monitor Countdown Deal performance (Book 1 is at $0.99)
- Prepare price-settle email (T+7): "The fire holds a price change" — Book settles to $4.99
- Continue Amazon Ads optimization if running
- Review-request follow-up to ARC readers who haven't posted

## T+7 (Monday)

- Countdown Deal ends; price settles to $4.99 per Board strategy
- Launch-week scoreboard post to Board: total downloads, rank peak, KENP total, reviews count, subscriber growth during launch week, ad spend + performance

## What's happening on Amazon's side (context for decisions)

- Pre-order sales count toward launch-day rank spike
- KU page-reads begin counting at T-0; don't count toward pre-launch rank
- Launch-week rank halo lasts ~7 days, then algorithm recalibrates based on sustained sales
- Also-boughts take ~2–3 weeks to stabilize; cover + blurb must match reader expectations to populate relevant also-boughts
- Category rank drives discoverability more than overall BSR

## Don't do on launch day

- Don't spam the list with more than 2 emails (one morning, one evening resend max)
- Don't switch platforms / post everywhere at once — concentrate on your strongest 2 channels (likely email + BookTok)
- Don't spend your entire paid-ad budget day one — pace across 7 days
- Don't reply to concerning reviews (bad reviews, AI-callouts). Silence + escalate to Board.
- Don't over-promise in subject lines. "It's open" outperforms "🔥🔥🔥 LAUNCH DAY 🔥🔥🔥"
