# Indie Cozy Fantasy Launch Playbook

Pre-launch (8 weeks) → Launch week (7 days) → Post-launch (30 days) → Series long-tail (ongoing).

Budget-conservative: organic-first, paid-second. Engagement-quality > vanity-count.

See `references/pre-launch-8-weeks.md`, `references/launch-week-choreography.md`, `references/post-launch-30-days.md`, `references/channel-economics.md`.

## Core principles

1. **The email list is the only asset you own.** Everything else (BookTok, Reddit, Amazon Ads, BookBub) is rented. Optimize for list growth + engagement; rented channels are inputs, not outputs.

2. **Engagement > count.** 500 engaged subscribers (30%+ open rate, 15%+ click rate on welcome sequence) out-perform 1500 unqualified ones. Launch-week BSR is driven by download velocity from the engaged tier.

3. **Launch-week velocity is the algorithm.** Amazon gives a 7-day attention spike based on first-week sales velocity. Your pre-launch job is to have a warmed-up list poised to push that window.

4. **Organic before paid.** You cannot read paid-ad attribution without an organic baseline. Measure organic CPA for 2 weeks minimum before touching paid.

5. **Pen persona is inviolable.** Never break it publicly. Silent stance on AI. Jason writes books; he doesn't run campaigns.

6. **Don't spend money you don't need to.** Every dollar has opportunity cost. Board approves over $50.

## Launch-week (7 days)

See `references/launch-week-choreography.md`.

- T-3: pre-order nudge email + final BookTok
- T-0: launch-day email, all reviewers notified, Amazon Ads campaign goes live (budget-gated), social push
- T+1 to T+3: daily monitoring, review-ask email, Countdown Deal price management
- T+3: review-request email to ARC team
- T+7: launch-week-close scoreboard; Countdown deal ends; price settles to $4.99

## Budget framework

- $0–$50/week: manager discretion
- $50–$200: Board-approved, written hypothesis
- $200+: Board-approved with ROI projection + kill criteria

## Hard rules

- Email list is the metric that matters
- Pen persona silent on AI / process / roster
- Weekly scoreboard every Monday without fail
- No paid spend without Board approval over $50
- Organic baseline before paid attribution
- Engagement metrics lead every report
